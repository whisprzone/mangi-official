How does it work?

mangi reduces food waste by allowing you to track nearby restaurants and cafes that have good but leftover food, which would otherwise be thrown away at the end of the day.

You can go to the restaurant to get the food or choose food delivery right to your doorstep.



What if the leftover food has gone bad?

mangi strictly requires all participating businesses and restaurants to only report and serve leftover food that is still good to eat, so you can rest assured that all food on this service is safe for consumption.
